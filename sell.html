Give Your Clothes a Second Life

Learn how easy it is to sell your pre-loved fashion with Urban Thread.

How It Works

1

Create Your Listing

Take clear photos of your items and provide a detailed description, including size, brand, and condition.

2

Set Your Price

Decide on a fair price for your items. You can browse similar listings for guidance.

3

Manage Your Sales

Respond to inquiries from potential buyers and arrange for shipping or local pickup once an item is sold.

4

Get Paid

Once the buyer receives their item, you'll receive your payment through our secure system.

Tips for Successful Selling

  • Use high-quality photos taken in good lighting.
  • Be honest and accurate in your descriptions.
  • Respond promptly to buyer inquiries.
  • Consider offering bundle deals for multiple items.
  • Package your items carefully for shipping.
