Why Donate Through Urban Thread?

Your donations help us support local charities and communities in need. By giving your pre-loved clothes a second life, you contribute to sustainable practices and make a real difference in people's lives.

  • Reduce textile waste and promote environmental sustainability.
  • Support individuals and families in need within your community.
  • Give your clothes a meaningful second chapter.
  • It's a simple and convenient way to declutter your wardrobe.

How to Donate

1. Gather Your Items

Collect the clothes you wish to donate. Please ensure they are clean and in good, wearable condition.

2. Package Your Donation

Pack your items securely in a bag or box.

3. Choose a Donation Method

You can either drop off your donation at one of our designated collection points or arrange for a pickup.
